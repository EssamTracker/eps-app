Complete User Guide
📋 All Tasks
●Type a task in the Quick Add box and press Enter to add it instantly
●Click any task to open its detail panel (title, priority, due date, assignee, notes, attachments, subtasks)
●Change task status from the detail panel: In Progress → Done
●Use Filter to narrow tasks by priority, status, or assignee
●Use Sort to order by priority, due date, status, or manual order
💡 In Manual sort mode, drag any task by its ⠿ handle to reorder it
🚨 Priority Levels
●Urgent 🔒 — Always pinned to the top, cannot be moved manually
●High — High priority task
●Medium — Default priority level
●Low — Low priority task
💡 Urgent tasks are locked 🔒 in all views (List, Kanban, Free Board) and cannot be dragged
👁 Task Views
●☰ List — Vertical list with drag-to-reorder support
●⊞ Kanban — Columns by status (Not Started / In Progress / Done). Drag a card between columns to change its status
●🎨 Free Board — Responsive grid. Drag cards to reorder. Click the ● dot on a card to assign it a color
💡 In Kanban, drag cards by the ⠿ handle to reorder within a column or move across columns
⏰ Today's Focus
●Open any task's detail panel and press Pin to Today to add it to your daily list
●Pinned tasks appear in Today's Focus so you can track them throughout the day
●Tap ✓ next to a task to mark it done for today
💡 The yellow badge in the sidebar shows how many of today's tasks are still pending
📒 Contacts
●Add Suppliers, Contractors, and Service Providers to your contact book
●Each contact stores: name, company, mobile, email, and specialty
●Search contacts by name or company name
●Press 🖨 to print the full contact list
👥 Team
●Add team members with their name, role, email, and phone
●Assign any task to a team member from the task detail panel
●Filter tasks by assignee from the All Tasks screen
📝 Notes
●Create notes linked to a specific project or standalone
●Each note can have a link and a due date
●Recent notes appear as a preview on the Dashboard
📅 Meetings
●Schedule meetings with a title, time, attendee, and linked project
●Supports recurring meetings: daily, weekly, or monthly
●Add an Outcome after the meeting to record what was decided
●Press ↗ To Task to convert a meeting outcome into a task directly
📁 Projects
●Create projects from + New Project in the sidebar
●Click a project name to view only its tasks and notes
●Tasks, notes, and meetings can each be linked to a project
●Press ⋯ on a project to edit, delete, or export its report
# Hashtags
●Type #word in a task title or notes to auto-create a hashtag
●Hashtags appear in the sidebar — click one to filter all related tasks
↕ Drag & Drop
●Use the ⠿ grip on the left of a card to drag and reorder it
●Urgent tasks are locked 🔒 and cannot be dragged in any view
●In Kanban, drag a card to another column to change its status
●The first drag automatically switches the sort mode to Manual
📊 Reports
●View task statistics: completed, in progress, and overdue
●Charts showing priority and status distribution
●Filter the report by project or date range
●Press 🖨 to print or save the report as PDF
📥 Incoming
●Manage incoming purchase orders (LPOs) and documents
●Each item is linked to a project and has a tracking status
💾 Backup & Restore
●Press the 💾 button in the topbar to open the backup panel
●Export — Download all your data as a JSON file
●Import — Restore data from a previously saved JSON file
⚠️ Back up your data regularly to avoid losing it
⚙️ Settings
●Press 🌙 / ☀️ in the topbar to toggle dark and light mode
●Open Settings from the sidebar to update your display name and preferences
●Trash — restore deleted tasks or permanently remove them
👑 Admin — User Accounts
Click Refresh to load data.
